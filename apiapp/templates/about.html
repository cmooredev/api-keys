How it works

The bot uses multiple translation services to get the most accurate result. First we detect the language and then route it through either deepL or Google translate, two of the most accurate translation services.

Adding the bot to your server.

After purchase, you will be provided a link to add the bot to your server. Make sure the server id matches the one you provided during purchase.

Once the bot is added to your server, users can use the command .trconfig to set their target language. You must also create a 'Translate' role and give it to all users who wish to be translated. If they do not select a language with .trconfig, the translator will by default translate into English

Commands

.trconfig : Bring up menu to select language.

.trstats : Check available credits.

New bots are being added regularly. Contact us for custom requests.
